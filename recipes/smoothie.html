Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Description

A cold, sweet, and slightly creamy strawberry banana smoothie made with strawberries, bananas, and vanilla yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups vanilla yogurt
  • 2 bananas, cut up
  • ½ cup frozen strawberries
  • 2 tablespoons wheat germ
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Steps

  1. Combine the yogurt, bananas, strawberries, wheat germ, and honey in a blender; blend until smooth, about 1 minute.
